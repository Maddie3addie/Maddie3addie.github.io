As the human population has grown, so has our impact on the planet.
Protected conservation areas are a necessary tool we must utilize, if we want to continue to have access to many of the things that make our planet a beautiful place to live.

Sadly, these protected areas face numerous challenges being implemented due to political pushback, as well as the unpredictability of the further damage climate change will cause to those areas in the near future*. Additionally, even if those areas are provided with a Protected Status, there is no guarentee that they will maintain their Protected Status in the future**.
